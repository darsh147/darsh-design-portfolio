Yes, if you hire me to host your site, I recommend setting up your own Hostinger account. This ensures you have 100% ownership of your domain and files. I will handle all the setup and deployment on your behalf.

That's perfectly fine! You can use my free prompts to generate the base code in Google AI Studio. If you don't know how to piece it together or fix the styling, you can hire me to do the fine-tuning for you.
